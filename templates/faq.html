Booking

What is your cancellation policy?

+

You can cancel your booking up to 48 hours before check-in for a full refund. Cancellations made within 48 hours will incur a one-night charge. For non-refundable rates, cancellations are not permitted.

How do I modify my reservation?

+

You can modify your reservation by logging into your account on our website or by contacting our customer service team directly. Changes are subject to availability and may incur rate differences.

What time is check-in and check-out?

+

Check-in time is 3:00 PM and check-out time is 11:00 AM. Early check-in and late check-out may be available upon request and subject to additional fees and availability.

Do you require a deposit?

+

Yes, we require a security deposit of $100 upon check-in, which will be refunded after check-out pending no damages or extra cleaning requirements.

Can I get a receipt for my stay?

+

Yes, you can access and print your receipt from your account dashboard. Receipts are also automatically emailed upon completion of your stay.

What is your pet policy?

+

We welcome pets up to 50 lbs for an additional $50 per stay. Please inform us in advance about your pet. Some restrictions may apply to certain room types.

Rooms

What amenities are included in the rooms?

+

All our rooms include free Wi-Fi, flat-screen TV, air conditioning, mini-fridge, coffee maker, safe, ironing facilities, and en-suite bathroom with premium toiletries.

Do you have accessible rooms?

+

Yes, we offer ADA-compliant accessible rooms with wider doorways, roll-in showers, grab bars, lowered fixtures, and visual alarms. Please request when booking.

Are pets allowed in the rooms?

+

We are a pet-friendly hotel! Pets up to 50 lbs are allowed for an additional $50 per stay. Please inform us in advance about your furry friend.

Do rooms have kitchenettes?

+

Our suites include kitchenettes with microwave, sink, refrigerator, and basic cooking utensils. Standard rooms have mini-fridges and coffee makers only.

What size are your beds?

+

We offer rooms with king beds (76" x 80"), queen beds (60" x 80"), or two double beds (53" x 75" each). Bed type can be selected during booking.

Is room service available?

+

Room service is available daily from 6:00 AM to 11:00 PM. You can order through our mobile app or by calling the front desk.

Services

Do you offer airport transportation?

+

Yes, we provide complimentary airport shuttle service from 6:00 AM to 10:00 PM daily. Please schedule your pickup at least 2 hours in advance.

Is there parking available?

+

We offer both valet parking ($35/night) and self-parking ($25/night) options. Electric vehicle charging stations are also available for guests.

Do you have a fitness center?

+

Our 24-hour fitness center features cardio equipment, free weights, yoga mats, and towels. Access is complimentary for all hotel guests.

Is there a swimming pool?

+

Yes, we have an outdoor heated swimming pool open from 8:00 AM to 10:00 PM seasonally (April through October). Pool towels are provided.

Do you have laundry facilities?

+

We have self-service laundry facilities on the 3rd floor open 24/7. Dry cleaning and valet laundry services are also available for an additional fee.

Is there a business center?

+

Our business center is open 24/7 and offers computers, printers, photocopiers, and office supplies complimentary for guests.

Payments

What payment methods do you accept?

+

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express, Discover), debit cards, and mobile payments like Apple Pay and Google Pay.

When is my credit card charged?

+

Your card is authorized at the time of booking and fully charged 48 hours before your scheduled check-in date.

Do you accept cash payments?

+

We accept cash for incidental expenses, but a valid credit card is required for the room reservation and security deposit.

Can I pay with multiple payment methods?

+

Yes, you can split your payment between multiple credit cards or combine credit card and cash payments at check-in.

Is there a resort fee?

+

Yes, we charge a daily resort fee of $25 plus tax, which includes Wi-Fi, fitness center access, pool towels, local calls, and newspaper.

Do you offer corporate rates?

+

Yes, we offer special corporate rates for business travelers. Please contact our sales department for more information and eligibility.

General

What is your smoking policy?

+

We are a 100% smoke-free property. Smoking in rooms will result in a $250 cleaning fee. Designated smoking areas are available outside the building.

What is the minimum check-in age?

+

Guests must be at least 21 years old to check in. Valid government-issued photo identification is required for all guests.

Do you offer luggage storage?

+

Yes, we provide complimentary luggage storage before check-in and after check-out at the front desk.

Is the hotel wheelchair accessible?

+

Yes, our hotel is fully wheelchair accessible with ramps, elevators, and accessible public areas throughout the property.

Do you have connecting rooms?

+

Yes, we offer connecting rooms for families and groups. Please request connecting rooms at the time of booking as they are subject to availability.

What languages do your staff speak?

+

Our staff speaks English, Spanish, French, and Mandarin to assist international guests with their needs.
